A Place That is a Strong Blend of Nature, Spirituality, and Adventure

Known as “Heaven on the Earth”, Jammu & Kashmir is where mother nature has bestowed all her love, it would seem. Enclosed by the Himalayas and Karakoram ranges, and adorned with verdant meadows, lush valleys, dense forests, and splendid temples, this union territory of India is a holiday destination that you will instantly fall in love with. A newly formed union territory, Jammu & Kashmir offers plenty of tour options for travelers of all kinds and forms an ideal destination for family tours, honeymoon tours and is also the perfect place for adventure enthusiasts with the majestic valleys of Kashmir also offer adventure opportunities like trekking and skiing. The picturesque Jammu & Kashmir is even home to the popular Hindu cave temples like Mata Vaishno Devi and the Amarnath Cave, this place is always on the radar of pilgrimage tourists from all over the world.

Stay in a houseboat or cruise through the Dal and the Nigeen Lakes with a Shikara ride, and experience the serene destination of this union territory unlike any other.
